Donations from supporters like you allow us to continue rescuing, rehabilitating and rehoming Welsh Corgis.*

*tails optional

Corgi & Critters N.E.T. Rescue, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Contributions are tax-deductible.

 

All contributions directly support and provide the best care possible for corgis and corgi mixes. This takes form in numerous ways, including veterinary care, heartworm preventative, flea preventative, etc. Operating costs are less than 10% because the only wages paid are in the form of puppy kisses!
