Happy Tales of Adoption

  • Valen's Story

    Valen has found his MOM!!! The competition was steep, but only Valen had the courage to ask Momma Becky if he could live with her and love her the rest of his life.....she said YES! He didn't even have to get down on one knee, although he did do a "Down" for her.

     

    Favorite hobbies: being the center of attention

    Could do without:

    Distinct markings: extremely short legs

  • Clara's Story

    Clara has "Left the Rescue"! Her real Mom & Dad adopted her in October of 2012. Clara was almost skeletal when taken into rescue, but after two whole months of R&R, she was ready to find her forever home. She had a long journey to find them, but she made it. What a wonderful new family!

     

    Favorite hobbies: snuggling, lap time, making conversation

    Could do without: loud ruckus

    Distinct markings: huge smile

  • Camigwen's Story

    Camigwen, now named Arial, is one happy little girl. She came to the rescue as an owner surrender that didn't have time for her. We almost lost her to a very nasty genetic condition called von Willebrand Disease, but with wonderful care from our vets, she made a full recovery and has now found her forever home. Very much a lap girl, Arial loves to be held like a baby on her back. She is a darling dog, so friendly and loving, but also has a playful, mischievous side. She likes to swipe the toilet paper off of the holder and prance around with it.

     

    Favorite hobbies: belly scratches, throwing her ball to herself

    Could do without: the cats rejecting her play attempts

    Distinct markings: full tail!

  • Ranger's Story

    Ranger came into rescue when he was just over a year old. He was adopted in February of 2012 and could not be happier to find his forever home in Austin, TX. He is a great guy who loves nothing more than to be around people. He enjoys traveling with his "pack," whether it be to the mountains, to the river, or out to his grandparent's ranch. While he loves staying active, he ALWAYS finds time for a good snuggle and a belly-up nap.

     

    Favorite hobbies: tug, learning new tricks, hiking, chowing down

    Could do without: bathtime, squirrels in his yard, nail trims

    Distinct markings: freckled right ear, one black whisker

  • Brandy's Story

    Brandy is a sweet girl who came into rescue because her family didn't have time for her. Boy how that has changed! She was adopted by a wonderful new family in August of 2012, complete with big brother Riley (another corgi).
